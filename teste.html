The problem

Yesterday's controls can't see today's risk.

Generative AI dissolved the data perimeter. Every employee now has tools that can move sensitive information out of your organisation with a single prompt, and the security stack you already own was never built to see it.

Invisible to your firewall

A prompt containing customer records or source code looks identical to ordinary encrypted web traffic. Network controls and legacy DLP pass it straight through.

No record, no accountability

When data is pasted into a public model, there is no log, no owner and no evidence. You cannot govern, or prove control over, what you cannot see.

Regulatory exposure

Under GDPR and the EU AI Act, undocumented AI usage is a compliance gap. The question is not whether it is happening, but whether you can demonstrate control of it.

The average organisation runs dozens of AI tools its security team never approved.

AI Shield closes that gap, turning unmanaged AI usage into a governed, documented and defensible part of your security posture.

Why legacy tools fall short

Purpose-built for AI. Not retrofitted for it.

Traditional DLP was designed for email, endpoints and USB drives. AI Shield inspects the interaction itself: the prompt, the upload, the paste, at the point where the risk now lives.

FAQ

Questions we actually get asked.

Do I need to involve IT to install it?+
No. AI Shield is a Chrome extension any employee can install in under two minutes. For company-wide rollout, you can push it silently via Google Workspace Admin without touching a single machine manually.
Does our data pass through your servers?+
Never. All scanning and detection happens locally inside the browser extension. The content of prompts, files and clipboard data never leaves the device and is never sent to AI Shield's servers.
Does it work on Edge, Brave or Arc?+
Yes. AI Shield runs on Chrome and all Chromium-based browsers, including Microsoft Edge, Brave and Arc. Firefox support is on our roadmap for Q3 2026. Most enterprise environments run Chromium, so coverage is typically complete.
Can I trial it without Google Workspace?+
Absolutely. Individual employees can install from the Chrome Web Store and start the trial immediately, no admin access required. The Workspace integration is only needed for silent company-wide deployment.
How does it help with GDPR Article 32?+
Article 32 requires "appropriate technical measures" to protect personal data. AI Shield provides documented evidence of those measures: every attempt to share personal data with an AI tool is logged, timestamped and exportable as an audit report for your DPA.
What is the minimum commitment? Is there lock-in?+
No lock-in. All plans are billed monthly and you can cancel at any time. Annual billing is offered at a discount if you want it, but it is never required. The 14-day free trial needs no credit card and cancels automatically if you don't upgrade.
Can AI Shield replace our existing DLP?+
AI Shield is purpose-built for the AI tool blind spot that traditional DLP wasn't designed for. It works well alongside your existing DLP for complete coverage. For SMBs without a DLP solution, it handles the most critical risk vector at a fraction of enterprise DLP cost.
What happens when sensitive data is detected?+
The employee sees a clear, non-alarming alert explaining what was detected and why it's risky. They can remove the content and continue, or cancel. Admins receive a notification and the event is logged with full context for coaching or reporting.
